About UMANG

Our Mission

At UMANG – Adding Life to Years, our mission is to bring care, connection, and joy into the everyday lives of senior citizens. We believe that ageing is not the end of activity, but the beginning of a new, meaningful chapter filled with learning, laughter, and companionship. We aim to create a space where every senior feels valued, supported, and loved, a place that feels like home, surrounded by people who care. Through our day care programs, wellness activities, and community engagement, we work to enhance quality of life and restore a sense of belonging and independence. At Umang, we go beyond traditional elder care. We don’t just look after our seniors, we celebrate them, their stories, and their wisdom. Because we believe in not just adding years to life, but truly adding life to years.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about UMANG

What is UMANG?

UMANG – Adding Life to Years is a day center for seniors in Pune. We offer care, community, and activities that strengthen body, mind, and soul – in a safe and warm environment.

Who is UMANG for?

Our offer is aimed at older people who don't want to be alone during the day, are looking for new contacts, or need support in everyday life. Also, relatives who are working find trustworthy care here for their parents or grandparents.

What services does UMANG offer?

We offer: Day care with games, exercise, and conversations. Healthy meals and snacks. Help with doctor visits, shopping, and other errands. Leisure and creative programs (music, art, yoga, etc.). Courses and groups where seniors can share their knowledge.

What does a typical day at UMANG look like?

A day with us begins with a warm welcome and ends with a smile. Between 9 and 5 PM there are varied activities: exercise, crafts, music, shared meals, and small celebrations.

Do relatives have to be present the whole time?

No. Our trained team lovingly cares for all participants. Families can bring their loved ones in the morning and pick them up again in the afternoon – completely worry-free.

What are the requirements for participation?

Seniors should be mobile or able to walk slightly. We ask for a brief get-to-know-you conversation to understand individual needs and create the best care plan.

How can one register or visit UMANG?

Very simple! Call us, write us an email, or fill out the contact form on our website. We are happy to arrange a free trial day.

Does UMANG also offer special programs on weekends?

Yes, special activities take place on Saturdays – for example, movie afternoons, parties, or outings.

Is there medical care on site?

We work with doctors and professionals who regularly stop by or are available when needed. Our team is trained to help immediately with minor health concerns.

What makes UMANG special?

The human being is at the center for us – with heart, dignity, and joy of life. UMANG is more than care: It is community, trust, and a second home.
